About Us

Delta Misr started in Egypt since 2008 distributors of Fresh Fruits and Vegetables we focus on achieving the highest standards of quality producer our products admired by customers worldwide. We utilize leading-edge production line technologies for sorting and packaging our fruit, including citrus, peaches, grapes, mangos, and more! We have been harvesting high quality of products from our local farms that are located in the finest agricultural area in Egypt. Delta Misr is develop the business to establish one of the top agricultural food exporting companies in Egypt. Delta Misr started operating its own packing station with capacity of citrus production 10,000 ten thousands tons annually and cold Stores capacity of 500 Five Hundred tons
